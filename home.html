Welcome To Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, formerly known as Ceylon, and officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in South Asia. It lies in the Indian Ocean.Sri Lanka is well known for its rich Buddhist culture as well as other religions. Being a religious country, Sri Lanka has many places with religious and historic significance, which attract tourists from all over the world.

Despite its small size, Sri Lanka possesses a high level of biodiversity and wildlife resources, and is rated among the 34 biodiversity hotspots in the world.She possesses nearly 1,600 km (994 mi) coastlines with tropical beaches which are popular among both local and foreign tourists.Most of the coastlines of the country are studded with varying coastal features such as bays, lagoons, sandbanks, and rocky headlands.
